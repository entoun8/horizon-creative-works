## Internal Launch Checklist

Use this checklist when preparing to announce your launch:

- [ ] Verify production URL is live and all pages load correctly
- [ ] Test contact form one final time (submit test inquiry, verify email received)
- [ ] Update all social media profile bios with website URL (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- [ ] Update email signature with website URL
- [ ] Prepare social media posts and schedule for optimal posting times
- [ ] Draft and send personal network email to warm contacts
- [ ] Post LinkedIn announcement and engage with comments
- [ ] Submit sitemap.xml to Google Search Console for SEO indexing
- [ ] Submit sitemap.xml to Bing Webmaster Tools
- [ ] Add website URL to Google My Business profile (if applicable)
- [ ] Add website URL to local business directories (Yelp, Yellow Pages, etc.)
- [ ] Monitor analytics after launch to track initial traffic
- [ ] Respond to any inquiries or comments promptly

---

**Tips for Successful Launch Announcements:**

1. **Timing Matters:** Post on weekdays during business hours (9 AM ‚Äì 5 PM) for maximum visibility
2. **Engage with Responses:** Reply to comments and messages quickly to build momentum
3. **Cross-Promote:** Share your announcement across all your platforms
4. **Ask for Shares:** Don't hesitate to ask friends and colleagues to share your post
5. **Monitor Analytics:** Track traffic spikes to understand which channels drive the most visitors
6. **Follow Up:** Post updates a week after launch highlighting any early wins or client testimonials
